The Blossom

A pass-off story, written April 4–17, 2008 on the Knoll Forum.

Note: This story was originally written in reverse chronological order.

January 15, 2214
23:04 GMT

The starship Blossom, in transit between Persephone Colony and Haphaestus Colony, collides with an unidentified object and explodes. All hands reported lost. Cause of explosion under investigation.

January 13, 2214
6:00 GMT

The Blossom leaves space dock 12 hours behind schedule, after experiencing hyper drive malfunctions. The malfunctions are addressed, but the Poseidon Group loses 60,000 dactares when it’s forced to ship medical supplies destined for Milica V via another courier.

January 12, 2214
18:00 GMT

Launch of the starship Blossom is canceled due to “hyper drive malfunctions” after first officer Tak Twisp discovers an implosive device attached to the hyper drive, set to explode once the ship enters hyper space. Security personnel begins an investigation.

January 12, 2214
16:56 GMT

[Recorded comm conversation between Tak Twisp, aboard the Blossom, and unknown individual, location unknown.]

Twisp: It’s in place. Inspection’s in an hour. This ship isn’t leaving port.

Unknown: You’re sure? You’re absolutely sure?

Twisp: It’s interstellar law, ma’am. If a device like that is found on a ship, everything stops, and the feds take over. We’re stuck. Absolutely certain.

Unknown: [pause] This is Poseidon, Tak. If they covered up the factory explosion on Hephaestus, they can bury this. Don’t-

Twisp: Ma’am, don’t worry. It’s well in hand. There are feds on the premesis. There are probably reporters here, too. This isn’t getting swept under the rug like Hephaestus.

Unknown: Do you know what they said, Tak? “Minor difficulties.” I lost-

Twisp: I know. Don’t worry. This is the end. The feds will examine the ship, and they’ll find everything. It’s almost over.

Unknown: [pause] Contact me if there are any complications.

Twisp: There won’t be.

[End of transmission.]

[Recorded comm conversation between Tak Twisp, aboard the Blossom, and unknown individual, location unknown.]

Unknown: -don’t give me that shit, Twisp.

Twisp: I’m not trying to be difficult, Ma’am, but I think that’s overstepping our auth-

Unknown: The Hephaestus Factory Satellite exploded, Twisp.

Twisp: (silence)

Twisp: What?

Unknown: I said the factory exploded, Twisp. Explosive decompression, due to a faulty D-12 interlock in the docking ring. The shipment of Corsemite detonated as soon as the compartment cycled.

Twisp: Yo-you’re lying. Poseidon isn’t processing Corsemite on Hephaestus. They-

Unknown: You better believe they are, Twisp. Poseidon’s backing the Forellian’s and their Campaign on the Chaos Marches.

Twisp: But Corsemite’s only used for antimatter gravity warheads…

Unknown: It’s not like the Forellians are interested in living worlds, Twisp. They don’t call them Locust for nothing.

Twisp: (silence)

Unknown: Twisp, if you don’t think you can do this, we’ll pull you out, I can have another agent on site in 6 hours.

Twisp: No, I’ll handle it.

January 12, 2214
01:50 GMT

A large quantity of crates marked as medical supplies arrive at Persephone and are promptly loaded onto the Blossom. A confirmation message is sent to Poseidon headquarters, with one addendum: a manifest from a mining operation on Herakles - known for its Corsemite deposits - listing several cubic tons of “miscellaneous equipment.”

A copy of the message and its attachment is sent via blind cc to a Visseran ship in orbit around Hera, which immediately drops into hyperspace.

January 10, 2214
07:38 GMT

(From PNN, The Pandora News Network, Sector Feed)

The Poseidon Group announced today that the Factory Satellite in orbit around Hephaestus suffered a catastrophic loss of all hands during the arrival of badly needed components for relief kits being shipped to victims of The Ares Consortium’s fierce war with The Locusts in the Region of Warped Space known as the Chaos Marches. The Poseidon Group has implicated the terror organization known as REMUS in such a “dispicable, heartless, and cruel act of malice”.

January 6, 2214 06:12 GMT

[personal log of Esperanza Verdun, Captain of the ACS Harpy]

Apparently we found the right hiding place. The moon dust must be thick enough to mask us after all. Good thing. Wouldn’t have wanted to miss this. We’ve been able to pick up their comms and get ours to and from as well. Just about perfect.

My man aboard the Blossom tells me they left five minutes ago for Hephaestus. And my… man on Hephaestus tells me they’re expecting more materials today. Unbelievable. Dresden’s lost his mind if he thinks no one’s going to notice. But then again, this is Poseidon we’re talking about.

[sigh] The Merovingian’s been here for three days, and it hasn’t moved. Sensors have been going nonstop, but they’re just floating there. They’re supposed to be watching Hades to make sure the Locust don’t rush the rift and make it through. They’re supposed to be watching for us.

In three days, fifty Locust ships have made it through. Fifty. We don’t have sensor logs. Can’t give away our position. But we have it all on camera.

And you know what? I’m gonna tape it when I twist that bastard Dresden’s head off his shoulders.
