Coalfire (Villain)

Name: Jackson Cassidy
Approach: Dampening
Archetype: Fragile
Description: Thin white male, about 16 years old; long, dark brown hair; torn clothing; always silhouetted with firelight; glowing orange eyes; phantasmal, flaming stag antlers hover near head.

Health: 20 + 5 × Number of heroes

Status

  • Green: d10
  • Yellow: d8
  • Red: d6

Qualities

  • Imposing: d10
  • Close Combat: d8
  • Ranged Combat: d8
  • Cursed by the Dark Earth: d8

Powers

  • Fire: d10
  • Infernal: d8
  • Speed: d8

Abilities

  • Horror of the Black Flame: (Terror of Inadequacy) Attack with Cursed by the Dark Earth with Max; Hinder each opponent that can see or hear target with Min
  • Hunger of the Dark Earth: (Scrambling Strike) Attack with Infernal; reduce target's quality dice by one size until your next turn
  • Rotwolves: (Alert!) Replenish minions up to number of heroes
  • Shadow Feint: (Cheese It!) Reaction, when Attacked, Defend with status die; if damage reduced to 0, move anywhere in scene
  • Visage of Rage: (Dismantling Jab) Attack with Imposing; remove all bonuses from the target
