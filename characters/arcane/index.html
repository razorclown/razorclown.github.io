Arcane

Name: Julian Wellesley
Background: Upper Class
Power Source: Mystical
Archetype: Armored
Personality: Jovial

Despite his posh, privileged upbringing, Julian Wellesley has been remarkably level-headed from a young age. He also demonstrated impressive study skills when he began independent studies after a brief stint in boarding school which Julian's parents try not to mention. From his tutors, however, Julian received such good marks that his parents let him choose his own courses of study.

Naturally, given such an opportunity, Julian sought out and hired the best teachers of the mystic arts. And since his parents have not specifically asked if he is studying to become a wizard, he has not informed them. Neither have they asked whether he is the costumed superhero Arcane. He is.

Principles

Mastery

You have thoroughly studied your own powers and are proud of your mastery of them. You understand a good deal about the metaphysics of your powers.

Action: Overcome, using powers in a new way, with Max. Hero points for all.

Minor Twist: How did your powers fail you in the moment?

Major Twist: What side effects are you suffering from your powers?

Youth

You have an innocent and cheerful outlook on most things, based on your upbeat personality and general lack of experience. You can slip into many situations that adults would have trouble with.

Action: Overcome a situation where your age or size help with Max. Hero points for all.

Minor Twist: Who has been put out by your overconfidence?

Major Twist: What person that you would hate to let down is now very disappointed in you?
