Katsuki Bakugo

Alias: Undecided (Several of his suggestions have been rejected by the teaching staff.)
Background: Unremarkable
Power Source: Genetic
Archetype: Blaster
Personality: Arrogant

Katsuki "Kacchan" Bakugo is one of the lead characters of My Hero Academia. I can't recommend the series highly enough.

Principles

Ambition

There is something you want, and you strive towards achieving your goals, no matter the cost. You see paths to victory that no one else will.

Action: Overcome a situation where someone else has given you a bonus from a Boost and use your Max die. You and each of your allies gain a hero point.

Minor Twist: How is the pursuit of your goals getting in the way of being a hero in this situation?

Major Twist: What did you just pass up or miss that could have helped you achieve your biggest goal at last?

Explosion

You have an affinity to or a love of explosions. You can interact with them with ease.

Action: Overcome a challenge involving explosions and use your Max die. You and each of your allies gain a hero point.

Minor Twist: What other energy/element is currently causing your powers to go on the fritz?

Major Twist: What source of energy/element is currently dampening all your powers?

Abilities

Green (d10 / 28–22)

  • Aerial Feint: (Exploit Vulnerability) Attack using Flight. If you Attacked or Hindered that target in your previous turn, use your Max die in this Attack.
  • AP Shot: (Precise Hit) Attack using Explosion. Ignore all penalties on this Attack, ignore any Defend actions, and it cannot be affected by Reactions.
  • Win by Saving: (Rally) Attack using Conviction. Other nearby heroes in the Yellow or Red zone Recover equal to your Min die.

Yellow (d8 / 21–11)

  • Cluster: (Area Assault) Attack multiple targets using Explosion, using your Min die against each.
  • DIIIIIIIE!: (Heedless Blast) Attack multiple targets using Explosion. Use your Mid die against each target. Take irreducible damage equal to your Mid die.
  • Explosive Speed: (Adaptive) Boost yourself using Agility, then either remove a penalty on yourself or Recover using your Min die.
  • Hand Grenade: (Imbue with Element) Attack using Gadgets. Use your Max die. If you choose another hero to go next, Boost that hero using your Mid die.

Red (d6 / 10–1)

  • Howitzer Impact: (Charged Up Blast) Attack using Exposion and at least one bonus. Use your Max+Mid+Min dice. Destroy all of your bonuses, adding each of them to this Attack first, even if they are exclusive.
  • I Just Wanna Say, I'm Gonna Win: (Canny Awareness) Overcome using Conviction. Use your Max + Min dice. Hinder all nearby opponents with your Mid die.

Out

  • Hinder with single Explosion die
