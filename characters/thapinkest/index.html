Name: Maisie Collins
Background: Performer
Power Source: Genetic
Archetype: Psychic
Personality: Jovial

From birth, Maisie Collins' skin was bright pink and her hair green. In her own words, she "had more of the Mutant experience than the Black experience." Her natural charisma helped her navigate the prejudice and distrust she faced, until she was outed as a psychic in her teen years. Maisie started a vlog discussing the painful experiences that followed, which quickly gained a following and became a hub of mutual support for Mutant youth. When veteran heroes also took notice and reached out to her, Maisie invited them to guest on her show; her interview series made her a genuine celebrity.

Maisie is trying her best not to be swept up in her sudden fame. She genuinely wants to help people — but more than that, she can't escape the feeling that what she's doing is somehow important. Maisie's precognitive abilities sometimes show her flashes, moments of a future in the midst of… revolution? Invasion? Whatever these images mean, they are of a world in need of the kind of unity she strives to create.

Principles

Business

You are an entrepreneur — running a business is an important part of your life and your identity. You have a base of operations that you can rely on for support.

Action: Overcome a situation related to your business or knowing locals with Max. Hero points for all.

Minor Twist: You're always looking at the bigger picture. How does that cause friction in the moment with your team?

Major Twist: Your business interests are in danger. Where are your priorities, truly?

Destiny

Signs and portents lead you toward an inevitable place in your life. You can always gain some measure of direction when needed.

Action: Overcome a situation related to your destiny with Max. Hero points for all.

Minor Twist: When omen of dire fortune did you just witness?

Major Twist: What heinous prophecy just came true?
