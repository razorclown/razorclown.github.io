Printable version (PDF)

Name: Dana Colson
Approach: Leech
Archetype: Indomitable
Description: Be on the lookout for a white female in her late 30s, approximately 5'10", dark brown hair. Last seen falling into a vat of chemicals. Suspect is armed and dangerous.

Health: 35 + 5 × Number of heroes

Officer Dana Colson was not one of the good ones. She was a fixer for the local elites, always glad to make their indiscretions disappear for a favor or a discreet stack of cash. When she got called out, she got aggressive, and when she got aggressive, she got physical.

And when that happened on a catwalk above a vat of chemicals, she and the lady from dispatch that had confronted her both went in. They both came out physically changed. The dispatcher came out with a heightened sense of duty — Colson, less so.

Abilities

  • Murder Cloud: (Unnerving Whispers) Hinder multiple targets using Toxic. Recover Health equal to your Min die. If you roll doubles, also Attack one of those targets using your Max die.
  • Slippery Eel: (Hypnotic Gaze) Reaction, when Attacked, Defend yourself by rolling your single Elasticity die. If this negates the Attack entirely, Hinder that target and Boost yourself with that same die roll.
