Science Fairy

Name: Heather, Clan Willowsbough
Background: Otherworldly
Power Source: Training
Archetype: Sorcerer
Personality: Inquisitive

The pixie called Heather is an enthusiastic student of Dr. Cassiopeia Reynolds, a scientist who stumbled upon a faerie enclave in a forest in England. A dryad, trying to protect the forest, cursed Dr. Reynolds to become a tree. When Dr. Reynolds convinced the fae that she meant them no harm, they helped her enchant a suit of cybernetic armor to prevent her further transformation, leading her to become the superhero Dr. Dryad. The fae were so fascinated by Dr. Reynolds' scientific approach, the doctor offered to give lectures on it. Heather was the first to sign up.

From the first class in the enchanted meadow, Heather was enraptured. She wove herself a tiny labcoat and fashioned smart-looking eyeglasses. She commissioned a set of beakers and vials from the clan glassblower. And she read every scientific document Dr. Reynolds recommended — and a few from more dubious sources. Her mystical nature gives her an unusual perspective on science and technology, and magic frequently leaks into her "experiments." In some ways, she is more of a science fan than a true researcher, but she learns quickly, and her controls are gradually becoming more rigorous.

Principles

Flora

You can identify any type of plant life and determine its origin in broad terms, such as Earth, alien, etc.

Action: Overcome with the aid of local flora with Max. Hero points for all.

Minor Twist: What grows out of your control?

Major Twist: How is nature reclaiming something important?

Science

You are up to date on and understand most modern scientific theories and research and can quote from them during conversations.

Action: Overcome with specific scientific principles using Max. Hero points for all.

Minor Twist: What were the surprising effects of leveraging that scientific principle in this situation?

Major Twist: Oh heck! What just blew up?
