Principles

Gearhead

You always know the general state of repair or function of an item of technology, whether it's a simple toaster or an alien orbital defense system.

Action: Overcome a technological challenge with Max. Hero points for all.

Minor Twist: What mechanical device just shorted out?

Major Twist: What machine just went terribly off the rails?

Hero

Because of your abilities, you have a calling to protect others.

Action: Protect innocent people in danger to Overcome with Max. Hero points for all.

Minor Twist: Your immediate need to help someone else causes you to drop the ball in your personal life. What was it?

Major Twist: You're given an ultimatum between your life as a hero and something else you value. What do you give up?
