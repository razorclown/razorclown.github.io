Introduction

Mission Briefing

Talon has stolen Doomfist's remaining gauntlet from Numbani. But before they can activate it, they must retrieve a critical component, locked away by Overwatch in a secret location.

You have been recruited to retrieve it first.

The World of Overwatch

Overwatch is set in an optimistic near future on the edge of falling into dystopia. It is a world of cybernetics, flying cars, teleporters, and global culture. It is a world recovering from a brutal war between humans and sentient machines.

The heroes of Overwatch are comparable to the street-level heroes of Marvel, such as Daredevil or Spider-Man. Each of them is engaging enough to star in their own monthly series, but none of them is powerful enough to hold off an army on their own.
