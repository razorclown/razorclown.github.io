Win the Crowd Cheat Sheet

Promo Styles

  • Attitude: intimidation, sarcasm, snark
  • Comedy: fun, humor, goofiness
  • Heart: heroism, determination, white-meat babyfaceness
  • Presence: look, aura, badassitude
  • Weird: supernatural, otherworldly, uncanniness

Fighting Styles

  • Acrobatic: leaping or flying attacks
  • Grappling: grabbing and moving your opponent
  • Power: raw strength over technique
  • Striking: punches, kicks, headbutts, etc.
  • Submission: inflicting pain to force your opponent to give up

Momentum

  • Spend before roll
  • Your roll: +1 for each
  • Opponent's roll against you: -1 for 2

Dice Results

Roll Result
12 or higher Critical: Succeed and give your target a twist, or succeed twice
10–11 Success
7–9 Partial Success: Succeed and take a twist
6 or lower Failure: Take a twist

Moves

Move the Audience

Result Options
Success Gain one Connection
Twist Lose one Momentum
  • Twice per session: once during battle scene, once during talking scene
  • Can't spend Momentum
  • Use bonus from previous roll
  • Record how you gained Connection

Cut a Promo

Result Options
Success Gain two Momentum
Get what you ask for
Twist Lose two Momentum
Get the opposite of what you ask for

Fighting Move

Result Options
Success Add one tick to a Finish clock
Twist Gain disadvantage against a fighting style until the end of the battle
Lose two Momentum

Set Up Move

Result Options
Success Gain +2 on your next Fighting Move
Gain one Momentum
Twist The next Fighting Move made against you gains +2
  • May use Fighting Style or Promo Style

Special Moves

  • Signature: Gain 2 Momentum on first time you inflict damage
  • Finisher: Do 1 extra damage for each success; if you don't end the match, target gains 2 Momentum
